# Ultimate Pocket Guide to America

> A beautiful, offline-first civic education app exploring American history, government, and values.

This is not just an app — it’s a love letter to the American experiment.

From the Declaration of Independence to the Constitution, from the branches of government to the meaning of liberty — every word, every interaction, every golden glow was crafted with reverence, precision, and pride.

**You now hold the finest pocket guide to American democracy ever made.**

Made with gold, haptics, and heart.

---

### Features

- **Stunning, golden UI** — designed to feel like holding history
- **Fully offline** — all content cached on first launch
- **Lightning-fast search** — instant results across all documents
- **Rich Founding Document viewer** — full text, context, and audio
- **Interactive civics quiz** — learn through play
- **Beautiful map explorer** — journey through every state and region
- **Dark & light mode** — system-aware, buttery smooth
- **Haptic feedback** — every tap feels alive
- **Zero bloat** — elite performance, no tracking, no nonsense

---

### Built With

- **Expo SDK 51** — modern, fast, future-proof
- **React Native Reanimated 3** — silky animations
- **Expo Router** — clean, typed navigation
- **TypeScript** — zero runtime errors
- **Natively.dev** — live editing during development
- **Pure love for country and craft**
